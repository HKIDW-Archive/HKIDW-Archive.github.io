A.I. Lab

Classrooms are often boring and rectangular, but Studio 93 has proven that this is not necessary. An entire room has been dedicated to artificial intelligence at Lok Sin Tong Yu Kan Hing Secondary School, where diagonal planes and lines cut across, deconstructing the space and giving it a futuristic appearance. These diagonal half walls create a sense of mystery, and then a thrill of discovery as everything comes into view. The open, light space is defined by geometric shapes, bright white walls with digital display panels for teaching, and a hardwood floor.
